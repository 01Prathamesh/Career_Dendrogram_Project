Welcome to the Research Scientist Career Path

A Research Scientist conducts experiments and studies to discover new knowledge in fields like biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science. It involves working with laboratories, experiments, and advanced research methodologies.

How to Become a Research Scientist after 12th Science

To pursue a career as a Research Scientist, students can study fields like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, or Environmental Science at undergraduate and postgraduate levels. A Ph.D. is often required for advanced research roles.

  • Skills Required: Strong problem-solving skills, scientific knowledge, critical thinking, attention to detail, and proficiency in laboratory work.
  • Key Industries: Pharmaceuticals, Biotechnology, Academic Research, Environmental Studies, Technology.
  • Popular Fields: Chemistry, Physics, Environmental Science, Biotech, Medical Research.
