Welcome to the 12th Arts Stream

Students pursuing the 12th Arts stream can explore a wide range of creative career options, including content creation. Content creators engage with audiences by producing videos, blogs, and social media posts.

With platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok, this career offers flexibility and creative freedom.

Content Creator Career Paths after 12th Arts

There are many paths you can take as a content creator:

  • YouTube Creator: Make videos on a wide range of topics, from education to entertainment.
  • Instagram Influencer: Create posts and stories, building a brand or promoting products.
  • Blogger: Write articles or blogs on specific niches like travel, lifestyle, fashion, or technology.
  • Podcast Creator: Host and create podcasts around topics you are passionate about.
  • TikTok Creator: Produce short-form videos for TikTok, often focused on trends, comedy, or advice.
