Welcome to the Environmental Scientist Career Path

Environmental Scientists study the environment and work to protect natural resources. They address environmental issues like pollution, conservation, and climate change.

How to Become an Environmental Scientist after 12th Science

Students can pursue undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in Environmental Science, Environmental Engineering, or related fields. This can lead to roles in government, private sectors, and research organizations.

  • Skills Required: Knowledge of environmental policies, scientific research skills, fieldwork, and data analysis.
  • Key Industries: Environmental Consultancy, Government Agencies, Non-profits, Research Institutions.
