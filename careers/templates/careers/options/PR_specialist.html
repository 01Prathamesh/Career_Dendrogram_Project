Welcome to the Public Relations Specialist Career Path

Public Relations Specialists manage an organization's reputation by communicating with the public, media, and other stakeholders. They create strategies for building and maintaining a positive image.

How to Become a Public Relations Specialist after 12th Commerce

Students can pursue a degree in Public Relations, Journalism, or Communications after 12th Commerce. A specialization in digital marketing, media management, or corporate communications can further enhance their skills.

  • Skills Required: Strong communication skills, writing, media relations, crisis management, and creativity.
  • Key Roles: PR Manager, Media Relations Specialist, Corporate Communications Manager, Content Strategist.
  • Key Industries: Media, Corporate Sector, Entertainment, Government, Non-profits.
