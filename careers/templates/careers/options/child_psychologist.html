Welcome to the 12th Science Stream

Students who pursue the 12th Science stream can enter fields that involve understanding human behavior and mental health. A career as a child psychologist is particularly rewarding for those passionate about helping children manage emotional, social, and psychological challenges.

Child psychologists work with children and adolescents to diagnose and treat emotional and behavioral issues, guiding them towards better mental health and well-being.

Child Psychologist Career Paths after 12th Science

After completing the 12th Science stream, aspiring child psychologists can pursue various career paths:

  • Clinical Psychologist: Assess and treat mental health conditions in children and adolescents.
  • School Psychologist: Work in educational settings to support students' emotional well-being and learning difficulties.
  • Developmental Psychologist: Focus on understanding childhood development, including cognitive, emotional, and social growth.
  • Behavioral Therapist: Work with children who exhibit behavioral issues, using therapy to promote healthier habits and responses.
  • Child and Adolescent Counselor: Provide counseling services to young individuals, helping them navigate personal and emotional challenges.
