Welcome to the Financial Analyst Career Path

A Financial Analyst analyzes financial data to help companies and individuals make investment decisions. They focus on budgeting, forecasting, and financial modeling.

How to Become a Financial Analyst after 12th Commerce

After completing 12th Commerce, students can pursue undergraduate courses in Finance, Accounting, or Economics. Postgraduate options include an MBA in Finance or a CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) certification.

  • Skills Required: Strong knowledge of financial markets, analytical thinking, proficiency in Excel and financial software.
  • Key Roles: Investment Analyst, Risk Analyst, Portfolio Manager, Financial Planner.
  • Key Industries: Banking, Investment, Insurance, Corporate Finance, Consulting.
