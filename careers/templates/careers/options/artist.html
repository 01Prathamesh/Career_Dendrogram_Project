Welcome to the 12th Arts Stream

The 12th Arts stream provides aspiring artists with the skills to create visual art across a variety of mediums. Whether you are passionate about painting, sculpture, digital arts, or mixed media, being an artist offers boundless creative freedom.

Artists play a vital role in cultural, social, and personal expression through their artwork.

Artist Career Paths after 12th Arts

After completing the 12th Arts stream, there are numerous paths an artist can explore:

  • Fine Art Painter: Create original paintings in various styles and sell them in galleries or online.
  • Sculptor: Work with materials like clay, wood, and metal to create three-dimensional art.
  • Digital Artist: Use digital tools to create artwork for the web, video games, movies, and advertising.
  • Illustrator: Create illustrations for books, magazines, advertisements, and more.
  • Art Curator: Organize and maintain art collections in museums or galleries.
