Welcome to the 12th Arts Stream

The 12th Arts stream provides a solid foundation for students who are passionate about teaching and art. As an art teacher, you will help others develop their creative skills, express themselves through various art forms, and appreciate the beauty of visual arts.

Teaching art involves guiding students through techniques in drawing, painting, sculpture, and other creative practices.

Art Teacher Career Paths after 12th Arts

Art teachers can work in various educational settings, including schools, colleges, and art institutions. Here are some potential paths:

  • Primary School Art Teacher: Teach young children basic art skills and foster their creativity.
  • High School Art Teacher: Teach advanced techniques and art history to high school students.
  • College Professor: Teach art at the college level, specializing in subjects like drawing, painting, or digital arts.
  • Private Art Instructor: Offer private art lessons to students of all ages, either in-person or online.
  • Art Workshop Instructor: Lead workshops for adults or children in community centers or art studios.
