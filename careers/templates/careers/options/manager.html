Welcome to the Manager Career Path

A Manager is responsible for overseeing and directing operations within a business or organization. Managers handle planning, organizing, and coordinating tasks to ensure the company's goals are met.

How to Become a Manager after 12th Commerce

Students can pursue a BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) or a B.Com with a focus on management. A Master's in Business Administration (MBA) is a common pathway to higher managerial roles.

  • Skills Required: Leadership, communication, problem-solving, and strategic thinking.
  • Key Roles: Project Manager, Operations Manager, HR Manager, Sales Manager.
  • Key Industries: Corporate Sector, Manufacturing, Retail, IT, Marketing, Healthcare.
