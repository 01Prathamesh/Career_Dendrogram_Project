Welcome to the 12th Commerce Stream

The 12th Commerce stream is designed for students who wish to explore fields related to business, economics, law, and finance. It includes subjects like Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies, and Mathematics (optional).

The Commerce stream provides a strong foundation for students to pursue careers in business, finance, law, and management.

Potential Career Paths after 12th Commerce

  • Commerce and Business Management: Students can pursue courses in Business Administration (BBA), Management (MBA), or Marketing Management.
  • Chartered Accountancy (CA): Students can pursue CA to become certified accountants.
  • Company Secretary (CS): A career in corporate law and governance as a Company Secretary.
  • Finance and Banking: Students can opt for finance-related courses such as B.Com (Finance), Financial Planning, or work in the banking sector.
  • Economics and Research: Students can explore careers in economics, economic research, or policy analysis.
  • Law: A career in law by pursuing a 5-year LLB program after completing 12th Commerce.
  • Government Services: Prepare for exams like UPSC, SSC, or state government services for various roles in the government sector.
  • Hospitality Management: Students can opt for a career in hotel management, event planning, or tourism management.
