Welcome to the 12th Arts Stream

The 12th Arts stream is ideal for students who are passionate about literature, writing, and creative expression. It offers an opportunity to dive into various writing careers, such as becoming an author, journalist, or editor.

Students in this stream develop strong communication and writing skills, which form the foundation for a successful career as an author or in other creative fields.

Author Career Paths after 12th Arts

As an aspiring author, you can write books, novels, articles, or poetry. There are several career paths in this field:

  • Fiction Writing: Write novels, short stories, or scripts in genres such as romance, mystery, fantasy, and more.
  • Non-fiction Writing: Write biographies, memoirs, self-help books, or articles based on factual information.
