Welcome to the Data Analyst Career Path

A Data Analyst uses data to help companies make informed decisions. The role involves interpreting large datasets, identifying trends, and creating reports. The field is essential in sectors like healthcare, finance, technology, and more.

How to Become a Data Analyst after 12th Science

After completing the 12th Science stream, students can pursue degrees and certifications in Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, or Mathematics to become a Data Analyst.

  • Skills Required: Strong analytical abilities, knowledge of programming languages like Python, R, and SQL, data visualization tools like Tableau, and statistical analysis.
  • Key Industries: Technology, Finance, Healthcare, E-commerce, Government, and Education.
  • Popular Tools: Excel, Power BI, Tableau, Hadoop, Python, SQL, R.
