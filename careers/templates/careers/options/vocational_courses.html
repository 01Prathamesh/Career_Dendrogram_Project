Welcome to Vocational Courses

Vocational courses provide specialized knowledge and skills in fields such as IT, hospitality, healthcare, beauty, and more. These courses are designed to offer practical training, which prepares students for immediate employment after completion.

The vocational stream opens doors to a variety of career opportunities, especially in sectors like healthcare, media, technology, and design.

Potential Career Paths after Vocational Courses

  • Healthcare and Nursing: A career in nursing, medical lab technology, physiotherapy, or pharmacy.
  • Information Technology (IT): Work in the IT sector in fields like programming, web development, and networking.
  • Hospitality and Tourism: Careers in hotel management, tourism management, travel agencies, and event planning.
  • Fashion Designing: Become a fashion designer, stylist, or work in the fashion industry.
  • Beauty and Cosmetology: Careers in beauty salons, makeup artistry, and cosmetology.
  • Graphic Designing: Work as a graphic designer, web designer, or in digital media production.
  • Automobile Engineering: Become an automobile technician or work in automotive industries.
  • Media and Entertainment: Work in radio, television, film production, photography, or video editing.
