Welcome to the Healthcare Career Path

The healthcare industry offers a variety of career opportunities for students after completing 12th Science. You can pursue roles like Doctor, Nurse, Pharmacist, or Medical Technician.

Healthcare Careers after 12th Science

Students can pursue a wide range of careers in healthcare after completing the 12th Science stream, especially by opting for medical or paramedical courses.

  • Medicine: Becoming a Doctor (MBBS, MD), Dentist (BDS), or Physiotherapist (BPT).
  • Pharmacy: Pharmacist, Pharmaceutical Scientist.
  • Medical Technologist: Medical Imaging, Lab Technology, Radiology, etc.
  • Healthcare Administration: Hospital Administration, Health Management.
