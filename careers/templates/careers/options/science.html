Welcome to the 12th Science Stream

The 12th Science stream is designed for students who wish to explore fields such as Engineering, Medicine, Research, and Technology. This stream covers subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics.

The stream offers a strong foundation for students aiming to pursue professional degrees or research in various scientific fields.

Potential Career Paths after 12th Science

  • Engineering: Students can pursue various branches like Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, and Computer Science Engineering.
  • Medicine: A career in medical sciences such as becoming a Doctor, Dentist, or Pharmacist.
  • Research: Research and development in fields like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Environmental Science.
  • Technology: Software development, IT consulting, and tech entrepreneurship.
  • Data Science and Artificial Intelligence: Work in data analytics, machine learning, and AI technologies.
  • Government Services: Entrance exams like IIT-JEE, NEET, UPSC, etc., for various government roles.
