Welcome to the 12th Arts Stream

The 12th Arts stream offers an excellent foundation for students interested in creative fields like graphic design. This career path focuses on visual communication through design software, artwork, and branding.

Graphic designers play an important role in industries such as advertising, web design, publishing, and more.

Graphic Designer Career Paths after 12th Arts

After completing 12th Arts, students can pursue a career as a graphic designer in various sectors:

  • Brand Design: Create logos, brand identities, and visual assets for businesses.
  • Web Design: Design user interfaces (UI) and websites, ensuring they are visually appealing and functional.
  • Advertising Design: Design posters, flyers, and advertisements for print or digital media.
  • Motion Graphics: Create animated graphics and videos for digital media.
  • Illustration: Create custom illustrations for books, magazines, and digital content.
